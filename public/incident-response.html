Duro Incident Response Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Goals

Detect, contain, and resolve security incidents quickly, and notify affected merchants without undue delay.

Process

  • Detect and confirm the incident.
  • Contain and limit impact.
  • Eradicate the root cause and recover.
  • Notify affected merchants as required.
  • Document and improve controls.

Contact

Report incidents via the support email listed in the app listing.
