Duro Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Summary

We collect the minimum data needed to connect to your Shopify store, generate content, and run the app. We do not sell personal data.

Data we process

  • Store details needed for setup (store domain, settings, and configuration).
  • Content topics and schedules you configure in the app.
  • Operational logs (system and access logs) for reliability and security.
  • App usage events needed to operate the service.

How we use data

  • Authenticate and connect the app to your Shopify store.
  • Generate, schedule, and publish content as requested.
  • Maintain performance, security, and reliability.
  • Provide support and troubleshoot issues.

Sharing

We use infrastructure providers to host the app. We share data with Shopify as required for app functionality. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service. See the Data Retention Policy.

Security

We use HTTPS in transit and encryption at rest when enabled. See the Security Policy.

Your choices

You can request data export or deletion through the app settings or by contacting us.

Contact

Contact us using the support email listed in the app listing.
