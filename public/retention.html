Duro Data Retention Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Default retention

By default, operational logs and activity data are retained for 90 days. The retention window can be configured by the app owner.

What is retained

  • System and access logs (for security and reliability).
  • App activity records (for audit and troubleshooting).
  • Configuration data required to operate the app.

Deletion

Merchants can request deletion of their data at any time. Data is deleted on uninstall or when explicitly requested.
